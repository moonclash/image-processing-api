The Good Morning Project

The Good Morning Project is a MaaS (Meme as a Service) project, specifically developed to solve a problem that many Eastern European mothers, aunts and grandmothers face every day - finding the perfect "Good Morning" picture to post on Facebook.

The Good Morning project solves that problem by providing a unique service, called GoodMorningfy

Simply upload a picture, and our state-of-the-art technology will turn your picture into a beautiful masterpiece, ready for your Facebook wall.
